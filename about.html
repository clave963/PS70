It showed in ten festivals globally in over five countries. <p class="margin"> </p> <div class="flexrow"> <img src="./Paar_01.jpg" alt="two dancers in the film paar" width=25%> <img src="./Paar_02.jpg" alt="the two dancers from above" width=25%> </div> <p class="caption">(Dancers: Sean Nederlof, Yasmin Schonmann)</p> <p class="margin"></p> I was an artist- in-residence with the Goethe Institute (2020-2021) as the artistic director of the Golem-Labor, an international workshop series researching motion capture technology with dance.
